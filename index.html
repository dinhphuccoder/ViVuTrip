Da Nang

Located in the South Central region, Da Nang has mountains, plains and sea. Famous tourist attractions when traveling to Da Nang include Ba Na tourist area, My Khe beach, and Asia's largest indoor entertainment area Fantasy Park. Da Nang also has many beautiful landscapes such as Hai Van Pass, primeval forests in Son Tra Peninsula and Ngu Hanh Son. In particular, Da Nang is surrounded by 3 world cultural heritages. Includes Hue, Hoi An and My Son, and a little further is Phong Nha - Ke Bang National Park. Every year, Da Nang organizes a Fireworks Festival that attracts many tourists from all over.
